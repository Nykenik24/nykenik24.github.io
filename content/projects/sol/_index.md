Sol is a Lua-inspired scripting language that _will_ have:
- Static typing.
- Structures.
- Arrays and maps separated instead of tables (doesn't seem like a breakthrough but trust me that being Lua-inspired that's weird).

It will be embeddable, trying to have low memory footprint, and also standalone.
